16. Volunteer Terms and Conditions

By signing up as a volunteer on the JUANBAYAN platform, you agree to the following terms and conditions:

  • 16.1 Volunteer Role:
    • 16.1.1 Volunteers are individuals who offer their time, skills, or resources to assist campaigns or initiatives hosted on JUANBAYAN.
    • 16.1.2 Volunteering is unpaid and is done on a voluntary basis without any employer-employee relationship with JUANBAYAN or its beneficiaries.

  • 16.2 Eligibility:
    • 16.2.1 Volunteers must be at least 18 years old or provide parental or guardian consent if below the legal age.
    • 16.2.2 Volunteers must complete the registration process and agree to JUANBAYAN’s verification requirements.
    • 16.2.3 JUANBAYAN reserves the right to approve or reject volunteer applications at its discretion.

  • 16.3 Responsibilities:
    • 16.3.1 Volunteers agree to:
      • Perform tasks to the best of their ability.
      • Comply with instructions provided by JUANBAYAN or the campaign beneficiaries.
      • Behave respectfully and professionally towards all individuals involved in the volunteering activity.
    • 16.3.2 Volunteers are responsible for their own safety and well-being during activities unless otherwise stated by the campaign organizer.

  • 16.4 Confidentiality:
    • 16.4.1 Volunteers may gain access to sensitive or personal information while assisting campaigns. Such information must be kept confidential and not disclosed to unauthorized parties.
    • 16.4.2 Any breach of confidentiality may result in the termination of the volunteer’s role and potential legal action.

  • 16.5 Reimbursement Policy:
    • 16.5.1 Volunteering is unpaid, and JUANBAYAN does not cover expenses such as transportation, meals, or accommodations unless explicitly stated and agreed upon in writing.
    • 16.5.2 Any reimbursements will be at the discretion of the campaign organizer and not JUANBAYAN.

  • 16.6 Liability and Insurance:
    • 16.6.1 JUANBAYAN does not provide insurance coverage for volunteers. Volunteers are encouraged to have personal insurance for their safety during volunteering activities.
    • 16.6.2 Volunteers agree to waive any claims against JUANBAYAN for injuries, losses, or damages incurred during volunteering activities.

  • 16.7 Termination of Role:
    • 16.7.1 Volunteers may terminate their involvement by providing reasonable notice to JUANBAYAN or the campaign organizer.
    • 16.7.2 JUANBAYAN reserves the right to terminate a volunteer’s role at any time if the volunteer:
      • Fails to comply with these terms.
      • Engages in unethical, illegal, or inappropriate behavior.

  • 16.8 Prohibited Activities:
    • Volunteers must not:
      • Solicit or accept unauthorized compensation from beneficiaries or donors.
      • Engage in activities that misrepresent JUANBAYAN or harm its reputation.
      • Exploit their volunteer position for personal or financial gain.

  • 16.9 Code of Conduct:
    • 16.9.1 Volunteers must adhere to JUANBAYAN’s Code of Conduct, which includes treating all individuals with dignity, respect, and fairness.
    • 16.9.2 Volunteers are expected to act in the best interest of the community and avoid conflicts of interest.
