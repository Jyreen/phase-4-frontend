Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome to the JuanBayan FAQ page, where you’ll find answers to common questions about our crowdfunding platform.

From events and campaigns to volunteer opportunities, funds, and rewards, explore how you can make the most of JuanBayan.

Events

To participate in an event:

  • Visit the event page on our platform.
  • Click on the "Join Event" button.
  • Fill out any required details and confirm your participation.
Campaigns

To create a campaign:

  • Sign in to your JuanBayan account.
  • Navigate to the "Create Campaign" section from your dashboard.
  • Fill out the campaign details, including title, description, target amount, and duration.
  • Upload images or videos to support your cause, then submit for approval.

The following terms apply for creating a campaign:

  • All campaigns must adhere to our community guidelines and policies.
  • The campaign must have a clear, measurable goal.
  • All funds raised must be used for the stated purpose.
Volunteers

To become a volunteer:

  • Create an account on the JuanBayan platform.
  • Visit the "Volunteer Opportunities" section and browse available roles.
  • Click "Sign Up" for the role you are interested in and complete the application.

Volunteering terms include:

  • Volunteers must be at least 18 years old.
  • All volunteers must adhere to the code of conduct provided by the campaign organizer.
  • Volunteers are responsible for their own travel and other personal expenses unless otherwise stated.
Funds

To withdraw funds:

  • Go to your dashboard and navigate to the "Funds" section.
  • Click on "Request Withdrawal" and specify the amount you wish to withdraw.
  • Ensure your withdrawal method (bank account or e-wallet) is properly set up.

If you are a donor and wish to request a refund:

  • Contact our customer support within 7 days of making the donation.
  • Provide proof of donation and the reason for the refund request.
  • Refunds will only be processed if the campaign does not meet its funding goal.
Rewards

Rewards can be earned by:

  • Donating to a campaign offering reward tiers.
  • Participating in promotional activities on the JuanBayan platform.
  • Referring friends to the platform using your unique referral code.

To claim rewards:

  • Go to your "Rewards" section in your account dashboard.
  • Review the rewards available for you to claim.
  • Follow the instructions to redeem or claim the rewards.

Rewards are non-transferable unless explicitly stated in the campaign terms. Always check the specific campaign rules for more details.
